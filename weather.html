Weather Concept App

"Since ancient, agriculture and weather is inseparable. Crop harvest and seasonal fresh food depend on the change in temperature and humidity. In modern society, humans no longer need to think about the food cycle as globalization and technology advancement eased the problem. This concept app combines the relationship between fresh food and weather, the suggestion of recipes for users based on weather, and aligns with business goals for promotion. The selected company is Woolworth, one of the largest supermarket groups in Australia."
