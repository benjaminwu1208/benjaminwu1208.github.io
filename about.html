Hello again!

I'd like to share with you my journey as an Experience Designer, let's get started!

What sparks me?

It all started with my internship experience at Injoy Health. I was working as an IT assistant, and my role was to develop the company website prototype and communicate with outsourced developers to make the design go live. Throughout my stay at Injoy Health, the term user experience was brought up during internal meetings over and over again, and yet I don't have a single clue on where to start with.

Then through my IT degree final year project, I was responsible for the frontend development and team facilitator to ensure our team can meet the client's requirements within the agile timeline and check with the client about the development outcome. I have developed a passion to create useful and aesthetic web interfaces, and I have also started to browse online materials about web designs and conventions.

After graduating with my bachelor, I decided to pursue a Master in Design to advance my design mindset and skills, and that's a wrap on the beginning of my design journey!

What's next?

With the master program, I was exposed to various design skills and techs: web and mobile designs, 3D (C4D), AR&VR (Aframe), etc, and also practiced my design and people skills to work with people from different backgrounds and cultures to co-create designs.

To date, I have recently completed the Academy Xi course with 2 successful client projects that are planned to ship in their future roadmap, these learning experiences enable me to contribute to projects that are going out to the public and make positive impacts.

What about now?

I am currently seeking job opportunities in the UX UI design field, and looking forward to co-design and co-create meaningful products to bring positive impacts to end users.

Feel free to connect with me via Linkedin!

Thanks!
