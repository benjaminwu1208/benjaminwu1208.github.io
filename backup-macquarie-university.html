Background

"To design a new user experience for onboarding/induction for Service Connect staff as we come together as a new entity ‘One Service Connect’ team by the end of 2022."

Project Overview

"There is a need to build the capability of Shared Services staff to deliver on the vision to improve the student and staff experience with shared services and transform to customer centric service orientated organisation. This aligns with pillar three in our operating plan Our People and Culture: Empowering our people to deliver optimal outcomes for customers and accountable for their individual and team development."
